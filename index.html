Products

Complete product portfolio across tissue, liquid biopsy, MRD, hereditary risk, and AI analytics

OncoGene TBX

Comprehensive DNA & RNA molecular profiling on tissue

  • Comprehensive DNA and RNA profiling from tumor tissue.
  • Detects SNVs, indels, CNVs, gene fusions, and splice variants.
  • Supports therapy selection and clinical trial matching.
  • High tumor-content sequencing for robust variant detection.
  • Ideal for initial diagnostic workup.

OncoGene LBX

Blood-based comprehensive DNA & RNA profiling

  • Non-invasive blood-based comprehensive DNA and RNA profiling.
  • Captures real-time tumor heterogeneity.
  • Enables treatment monitoring and resistance detection.
  • Useful when tissue is limited or unavailable.
  • Faster turnaround for dynamic treatment decisions.

OncoGene MRD

Methylation-based ctDNA MRD detection and response tracking

  • Ultra-sensitive methylation-based ctDNA detection.
  • Detects minimal residual disease at very low levels.
  • Enables early relapse prediction.
  • Monitors treatment response longitudinally.
  • Suitable for post-therapy surveillance.

OncoGene Heme

Comprehensive DNA & RNA profiling for hematological cancers

  • Comprehensive DNA and RNA profiling for blood cancers.
  • Detects fusion genes, clonal evolution, and risk markers.
  • Supports diagnosis, prognosis, and therapy stratification.
  • Covers AML, ALL, MDS, MPN, and lymphomas.
  • Enables MRD and relapse assessment.

OncoGene Hereditary

Comprehensive germline DNA testing for inherited cancer risk

  • Comprehensive germline DNA testing for inherited cancer risk.
  • Covers key hereditary cancer genes for major cancer types.
  • Identifies pathogenic variants for risk stratification.
  • Supports preventive screening and family counseling.
  • Informs personalized surveillance and prophylactic strategies.
